How to Get Your Spotify Data

Don't have your data yet? Here's how to request it:

  1. Go to spotify.com/account/privacy
  2. Scroll down to "Download your data"
  3. Select "Extended streaming history" (recommended for full analysis)
  4. Click "Request data"
  5. Wait 5-30 days for Spotify to prepare your data
  6. Download the ZIP file when you receive the email
  7. Extract the JSON files and upload them here

Note: Extended streaming history includes your complete listening history since you created your account. Standard data only includes the last year.
